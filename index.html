义眼

Artificial Eye

这是一颗仿真电子眼球，置入眼窝内使用。它可以拍摄图片和录制视频，并自动通过周围设备将内容加密上传到分布式云端。

An artificial electronic eyeball, implanted in the eye socket, can capture photos and record videos, automatically encrypting the content and uploading it to the distributed cloud via surrounding devices.

它包含一个内置电路的义眼台，以及一片带有透明孔洞的义眼片。两者之间通过磁力吸附定位。

It consists of a prosthetic eye table with built-in circuitry, and a prosthetic eye piece with transparent holes, which are positioned magnetically between them.

义眼台结构

义眼台内置：控制板、摄像头、电池、通讯天线、无线充电线圈等部件。使用时将义眼台置入眼窝内，然后将义眼片吸附定位在其前方。

The prosthetic eye table contains: control board, camera, battery, communication antenna, wireless charging coil and other components. To use the prosthetic eye table into the eye socket, and then the prosthetic eye piece adsorption positioning in front of it.
